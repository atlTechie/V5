Official Rules, Terms and Conditions of the Rewards Program

  • Points are valid 90 days from the date of issuance.
  • Participation is open to qualified participants selected by the RewardTrax® team.
  • Qualified participants will be enrolled by RewardTrax® representatives.
  • Participants must be in good standing with RewardTrax® in order to receive rewards.
  • Account statements and current point balance are available through the program website.
  • Rewards Program is void where prohibited by law.

Additional Rules and Guidelines for Rewards Program

  • In all matters relating to the interpretation and application of the program rules, the decisions of RewardTrax® will be final.
  • RewardTrax® reserves the right to change, cancel or extend the program at any time.
  • RewardTrax® reserves the right to audit all Program records at anytime.
  • RewardTrax® reserves the right to disqualify participants from eligibility for rewards and revoke any reward points issued.
  • The participant is responsible for federal, state and local income taxes on all rewards earned. RewardTrax® must complete an IRS form 1099 and send it to you for any year in which your reward fulfillments are valued more than $600. It is suggested that each reward earner consult a tax advisor in regard to reporting the reward income for income tax purposes. Any liability for federal, state or local income tax imposed on any reward earner will be the responsibility of the reward earner and not RewardTrax or Super Rewards Program Headquarters.
