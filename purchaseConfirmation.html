Purchasing Information

The points required to purchase your items will be deducted from your account balance.

Here's how our order processing works:

  • First, we convert your points to a currency equivalent. For example, we may convert your points into a US dollar ($) equivalent.
  • Next, using a background program account for currency transactions, we make the purchase of your item from a supplier on your behalf, for instance in dollars.
  • Then, we subtract the corresponding number of points from your point account. The items are shipped to you directly from the supplier.
